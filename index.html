Tandoori Momos

Tandoori momos are a delicious and popular variation of momos, where the dumplings are marinated in a tandoori spice mixture and then cooked in a tandoor or oven.

Pani Puri

Pani Puri consists of hollow, crispy puris (fried dough balls) filled with a tangy and spicy mixture of flavored water, mashed potatoes, chickpeas, onions, and chutneys.

South Indian Thali
